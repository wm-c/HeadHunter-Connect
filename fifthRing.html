Company Overview

Fifth Ring Leadership provides customized retained search services with a focus on small to mid-sized clients across Canada. Through our network of experts, we help our clients reach their goals through talent acquisition, coaching, leadership development and other business advisory services. Every situation is unique and there is no “one-size-fits-all” approach - we try to be adaptable to the needs of our clients. Whether you're interested in engaging in a formal search project, if you just want to talk about your situation for ideas, or if you have something else in mind - it starts with a conversation.

  • Founded in 1999.
  • Specializes in executive roles primarily focused on industry and manufacturing clients.
  • Looking for partner firms to expand executive search range.
